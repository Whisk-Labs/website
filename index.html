The Continuous Decision Loop

Seven steps that transform food operations from guesswork to intelligence. Each cycle makes the next one more accurate.

Step 01

Data Integration

Connects to your POS (Toast, Square, Lightspeed) and inventory platforms via API.

  • No manual data entry required
  • Setup in minutes, not weeks
Clean, unified data feed
Step 02

Demand Forecasting

ML models analyze sales history with day-of-week, holidays, weather, and local events.

  • Item-level accuracy
  • Contextual signals built in
Per-menu-item demand prediction
Step 03

Labor Scheduling

Turns hourly demand forecasts into shift coverage by role, aligned to rush windows.

  • Staff to predicted covers, not gut feel
  • Reduce over- and under-staffing
Role-based labor plans
Step 04

Inventory Optimization

Demand forecast x recipe mappings, compared against on-hand stock levels.

  • Auto-generate purchase orders
  • Right-size every order
Precise PO recommendations
Step 05

Prep Planning

Data-driven quantities for each menu item to batch-cook or prep per shift.

  • Reduce overproduction
  • Staff knows exactly what to prep
Shift-level prep guides
Step 06

Waste Logging

End-of-day: staff log unsold items and spoiled ingredients via a simple mobile UI.

  • Under 60 seconds per day
  • Closes the feedback loop
Real-time waste tracking
Step 07

Model Retraining

Waste and actuals feed back into the system. Models learn where you systematically over- or under-estimate.

  • Forecasts improve every cycle
  • Location-specific learning
Self-improving intelligence
